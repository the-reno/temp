About

The Rōnin

Mathematics graduate turned FX trader. I work on the foreign exchange desk at ANZ Bank in New York, pricing and managing swap deals — where calculus meets counterparty risk at the speed of a verbal quote.

Outside the desk, I build things: trading automation tools, data visualizations, interactive simulations. Anything where a mathematical idea can be made tangible through code.

The 浪人 (rōnin) concept fits: a lone practitioner applying craft without institutional permission — in markets, in code, in ideas.
